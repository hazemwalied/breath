About the product

Breath is a 100% natural and safe solution that helps cleanse the nose from impurities and pollutants, promotes respiratory health. It works with a balanced saline solution that is compatible with the body's natural composition, making it suitable for the whole family, including children, pregnant women, and breastfeeding women.

A 100% safe and natural solution, free of drugs or chemicals, just a balanced saline solution that matches the body's natural composition.
Daily support for nasal health. Regular use helps keep the nose clean and free of pollutants and microbes, reducing seasonal allergy and cold attacks.
Safe for all family members, it can be used by children and adults completely safely.
Safe during pregnancy and breastfeeding, free from any harmful substances or chemicals.
Distinctive and user-friendly design makes it easy to hold and operate with one hand.
Easy to clean and sterilizeu, Its parts can be disassembled and washed easily to ensure Maximum cleanliness.

How to use ?

1- Boil water for five minutes

2- Add a sachet of saline

3- Shake the device after closing it tightly.

4- Place the nazal nozzles outlet.

5- Press the button at the bottom of the bottle.

6- Repeat the process in the other nostril.

7- Note: (Keep your mouth open during use.)
